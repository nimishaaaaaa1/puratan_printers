MOO.COM & PURATAN PRINTERS DESIGN ANALYSIS

1. VISUAL HIERARCHY
   Key Elements:
   - Clean White Space
     • Generous padding between sections
     • Breathing room around content
     • Clear content separation
   
   - Typography
     • Headlines: Cormorant Garamond for traditional touch
     • Body: Open Sans for modern readability
     • Clear hierarchy (H1 > H2 > body text)
     • Size scale: 48px/32px/24px/16px
   
   - Color Palette
     • Primary: Deep Maroon (#8B0000) - traditional printing
     • Secondary: Antique Gold (#CFB53B) - heritage accents
     • Background: Parchment White (#F5F2E8)
     • Text: Rich Black (#0A0A0A)
     • Accent: Sage Green (#8BA888) - eco-friendly touch
